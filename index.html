Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to our Quran application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

a) Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location solely to determine the Qibla direction (the direction of the Kaaba in Mecca). We do not store, transmit, or share your location data with any third parties. Location access is used in real-time and only when you open the Qibla feature.

b) Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage and interaction data
  • General location (country/region level)

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can review Google's data practices at: policies.google.com/privacy

c) Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Account or login credentials
  • Payment information
  • Contacts or messages

3. Internet Access

Some features of the App require an internet connection (such as loading ads). Other features — including the Mushaf (Quran reading), Azkar, Duaa, Tasbih counter, and Names of Allah — are available fully offline and do not require internet access.

4. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed for a general audience and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. The App does not require registration or the submission of any personal information to use its core features.

Regarding advertising shown to children, we comply with applicable laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and configure our ad settings accordingly to ensure child-safe ad content.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. See their privacy policy at: support.google.com/admob

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect any information processed through the App. Since the App does not collect or store personal data on our servers, the risk to your personal information is minimal. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to us.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Location Access: You can revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The rest of the App will continue to function normally.
  • Ad Personalization: You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).
  • Reset Advertising ID: You can reset your Advertising ID at any time through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted within the App or on our app store listing with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
